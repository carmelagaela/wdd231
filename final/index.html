Featured safety tips

Keep track of doses

Maintain a medication log to avoid accidental double-dosing. Use reminders when necessary.

Store correctly

Store medicines at recommended temperatures and away from children and pets.

Dispose safely

Do not flush medicines. Follow local disposal guidelines or pharmacy take-back programs.
